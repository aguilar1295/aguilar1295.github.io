✪ About Blankuru:

Blankuru is a Mexican digital artist. He was born in Aguascalientes, Aguescalientes, México on October 30th, 2000. Once Blankuru and his family moved to the U.S., he discovered that he had a passion for illustrating, writing, and innovating. He and his sister, Abril, would spend hours drawing together and developing their own stories for the majority of their childhood.
Today, Blankuru is often found working on various projects. Of these projects, some of them include drawing on the iPad, working on a webcomic, and helping to run an online community for ARGs!

✪ About Blankuru's Art:

Blankuru prefers to draw religious subjects. Many of his illustrations aim to capture the struggle between biblical interpretations of good and evil. Blankuru also enjoys making illustrations of his family - more often than not, of his mother, Heidi.

✪ Tools Of The Trade:

Blankuru uses his school issued iPad to create his illustrations. He usually uses the Sketchbook App to draw. When Blankuru is not working on a new digital drawing, he can also be found doodling in his sketchbook.
